About Rexburg, Idaho

Welcome to Rexburg, a charming city in the beautiful state of Idaho. Explore the city's demographics, statistical data, upcoming events, and popular attractions!

Demographics

  • Population: 28,000
  • Average Age: 24
  • Ethnic Diversity: 90% Caucasian, 5% Hispanic, 5% Other

Statistical Data

  • Area: 4.6 square miles
  • Median Income: $30,000
  • Education: 95% high school graduates, 40% college graduates

Upcoming Events

  • Rexburg Farmer's Market - Every Friday, 3 PM - 7 PM
  • Rexburg Music Festival - July 15-17
  • Rexburg Rodeo Days - August 20-22

Attractions

  • Rexburg Nature Preserve: Explore scenic trails and local wildlife
  • BYU-Idaho: Visit the campus of Brigham Young University-Idaho
  • Teton Lakes Golf Course: Enjoy a round of golf with stunning mountain views

Rexburg is Home to all

Community-Centered and Friendly

Rexburg, Idaho is home to a warm and welcoming community. The people of Rexburg are known for their friendly and helpful nature. Whether you're a long-time resident or a newcomer, you'll quickly find yourself embraced by the tight-knit social fabric of the city. Neighbors often come together to support local events, volunteer for causes they're passionate about, and offer a helping hand to those in need. The strong sense of community in Rexburg is a testament to the residents' values of unity and inclusiveness.


Diverse Yet Connected

Rexburg boasts a diverse population, attracting individuals from all over the United States and even international backgrounds, thanks in part to the presence of Brigham Young University-Idaho. The city's residents come from various cultural, religious, and ethnic backgrounds, adding to the rich tapestry of Rexburg's social landscape. This diversity is celebrated and has led to a sense of open-mindedness and cultural appreciation among the locals. The people of Rexburg often participate in cultural exchange events and activities, fostering a sense of global understanding within the community.


Driven and Ambitious

Rexburg's residents are known for their strong work ethic and ambition. Many are students at Brigham Young University-Idaho, dedicated to their education and career goals. The city's entrepreneurial spirit is also evident, with numerous small businesses and startups thriving in the area. This ambition is balanced with a love for the outdoors, with residents frequently exploring the beautiful natural landscapes that surround Rexburg, including Yellowstone National Park. The people of Rexburg find inspiration and balance in their connection to nature, contributing to a holistic and fulfilling lifestyle.
