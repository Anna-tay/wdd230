Community Combers

Rexburg has many small businesses that have a big impact on its city. This site is here to give those a voice and let them know. help improve the city. This site will help them know and show their business.

Site Purpose

To promote businesses bit and small. This site will show to businesses and people when activities that Rexburg puts on where they can promote their business. It will also help with hiring new employees.

Scenarios

- What seasonal events are held?
- What activities are on short notice? What activities take a longer time to plan?
- Where can I find the most popular actives that happen in Rexburg?
