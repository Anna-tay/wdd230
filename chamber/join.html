Application Information

Membership Levels

NP Membership

Description: Membership is for non-profit organizations, and there is no fee.

Benefits:

  • Access to our online community and discussion forums.
  • Subscription to our newsletter.
  • Listing in our directory of non-profit organizations.
  • Opportunity to participate in volunteer programs.

Bronze Membership

Description: Our basic membership level with some added benefits.

Benefits:

  • All NP Membership benefits.
  • Invitation to our annual networking event.
  • 10% discount on tickets to our fundraising events.
  • Monthly spotlight position on our website's "Member of the Month" section.

Silver Membership

Description: A step up from Bronze with more benefits.

Benefits:

  • All Bronze Membership benefits.
  • Priority access to training workshops and webinars.
  • 20% discount on tickets to our fundraising events.
  • Quarterly spotlight position on our website's home page.
  • Exclusive access to our members-only resources.

Gold Membership

Description: Our premium membership level with the most benefits.

Benefits:

  • All Silver Membership benefits.
  • Complimentary admission to all our special events.
  • 30% discount on tickets to our fundraising events.
  • Bi-weekly spotlight positions on our home page.
  • Priority access to advertising opportunities on our website.
