Lizzy Mcbride

Embarking on the journey of starting a small business from home while expecting a little one is a unique and empowering experience. As a new mom-to-be, the initial stages of pregnancy often bring a surge of creative energy and a desire to create something meaningful. For me, it was the perfect time to turn my passion for crafting into a home-based business. Fueled by the anticipation of motherhood, I began handcrafting adorable stuffed animals and soothing rice packs, envisioning them as comforting companions for both children and fellow moms. The process of building my business became a therapeutic outlet , allowing me to channel the excitement and anticipation of becoming a mom into tangible, heartfelt creations.

As the pregnancy progressed, so did my business aspirations. The rhythmic stitching of stuffed animals and the careful selection of fabrics became intertwined with the preparations for the arrival of my little one. What started as a personal project soon evolved into a fledgling business. Friends and family were enthusiastic about the idea of handmade stuffed animals and rice packs, and their encouragement spurred me to launch an online store. The flexibility of working from home was a blessing, especially during the final trimester when rest became a priority. I could manage orders, create new designs, and build my brandâ€”all while nesting and eagerly awaiting the arrival of my bundle of joy.

Now, as a new mom navigating the delicate balance of motherhood and entrepreneurship, my small business has become a cherished part of our family's story. The gentle warmth of the rice packs offers comfort during late-night feedings, and the soft embrace of the stuffed animals has a calming effect on both my baby and customers alike. The business has grown, and with the support of my community, I've found a rhythm that allows me to continue pursuing my entrepreneurial dreams while savoring the precious moments of motherhood. Each stuffed animal I create is not just a product; it's a piece of the journey that started with a creative spark during pregnancy and continues to bring joy to families, including my own.
