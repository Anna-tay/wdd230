Simplicity

TechCo Solutions

techcosolutions.com

Reason why

This website is not too busy. For the into it is one image that has few words on it. As you go through the website, the colors and fonts are very simple. The content is also easy to understand and is presented in a short form, with bullets and simple headings.

Hick's Law

Amazon

Amazon.com

Reason why

Hick's law is all about smooth users experience, for Amazon, they have put all what they think the user will use the most at the top of their screen with good headers for all of the different places for amazon. As you scroll they populate the screen with the item they think you will want or the items most popular. To them let the user see the items they might want first. Also for the items they sale, they have a picture, price and the title of the item, so you can see it clearly.

Visual Hierarchy

Apple

Apple.com

Reason why

When you first open up the website, you see what they want you to see first. What they think you want to see first, with a little animation to draw your attention. This is a great way to pull users attention to the stuff they are selling. As you scroll down the website this same pattern appears. They put pictures of the products they are selling in a big font. They are making the products and names big, and the details of those products(price) small.
